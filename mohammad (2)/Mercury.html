Potential for Life?

Mercury has little potential for life due to extreme temperatures, lack of atmosphere, and no liquid water.


What is the gravity on Mercury compared to Earth?

Mercury's gravity is about 38% of Earth's gravity. This means you would weigh less than half of what you do on Earth.


average distance between the Mercury and the Sun?

surface of Mercury :
Mercury's surface is rocky, cratered, and has extreme temperature changes.
Long sunsets:
Due to its slow rotation, a sunset on Mercury lasts about 90 Earth days
Lack of atmosphere:
Mercury's thin atmosphere can't trap heat, contributing to the planet's extreme temperature swings.





why difficult explore Mercury


Exploring Mercury is tough due to extreme temperatures, radiation, and its elliptical orbit.
The lack of atmosphere makes landing hard,
