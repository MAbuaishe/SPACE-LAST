What is the Mars atmosphere made of?

carbon dioxide (95%), with small amounts of nitrogen (2.7%) and argon (1.6%).


Potential for Life?

Scientists are searching for signs of ancient life on Mars, when it was warmer and had water.


What is the gravity on Mars compared to Earth?

Mars has about 38% of Earth's gravity, meaning you would weigh less on Mars than on Earth.

surface of Mars:
Mars' surface features volcanoes, valleys, ice caps, deserts, and ancient riverbeds, suggesting past water flow.
Mars has two small moons:
Phobos and Deimos They're potato-shaped because they have too little mass for gravity to make them spherical.
Mars called the "Red Planet"?:
because its surface contains iron oxide (rust), giving it a reddish appearance when viewed from space.





explore Mars


Mars is explored using rovers like Curiosity and Perseverance,
which study the surface, and orbiters like Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, which monitor the planet.
Future missions aim to explore Mars for signs of life and potential colonization.
